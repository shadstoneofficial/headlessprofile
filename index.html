How to link it to your domain:

  1. Upload your downloaded files to a pinning service like Fleek or Pinata.
  2. Copy the resulting IPFS CID (Content Identifier).
  3. Go to HeadlessDomains.com and manage your domain.
  4. Add a new TXT record: _dnslink=/ipfs/<YOUR_CID>
  5. Your domain will now natively resolve to your decentralized profile!
